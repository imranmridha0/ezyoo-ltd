About EAZYOO

Our Story

Born From a Simple Idea

EAZYOO was founded with one clear mission: to create everyday products that are smarter, more portable, and built to last — without the premium price tag.

We noticed something simple — the products we use every day, from water bottles to cutting boards, take up way too much space. So we asked: "What if they could fold?"

That question led to our first two products: a collapsible running water bottle designed for athletes and adventurers, and a foldable cutting board for space-conscious kitchens. Both available on Amazon UK, both loved by our customers.

Based in the United Kingdom, we're a growing brand with big ambitions. We're expanding our range, partnering with retailers, and building a community of people who believe that smarter design leads to smarter living.
